About Us

Sherry International is a group company specializing in the global automotive sales ecosystem. Sherry International is based in Hong Kong SAR, China. The company aims to connect the automotive industry chain, building a global marketing network to become a major international player in automotive sales.

Sherry International has already established automotive marketing networks in multiple global markets and is rapidly expanding.

Mission

Sherry International is dedicated to creating a new ecosystem for overseas automotive marketing, becoming a model enterprise for Chinese auto sales abroad.

Vision

To become an innovation-driven leader in the overseas automotive marketing ecosystem.

Values

Integrity, Innovation, Focus, Craftsmanship.

关于我们

Sherry International 是一家专注于全球汽车销售生态系统的集团公司，总部位于中国香港特别行政区。公司旨在连接汽车产业链，建立全球营销网络，成为国际汽车销售的重要参与者。

Sherry International 已在多个全球市场建立了汽车营销网络，并正在快速扩展。

公司使命

Sherry International 致力于为海外汽车营销创建一个新生态系统，成为中国汽车在海外销售的典范企业。

愿景

成为海外汽车营销生态系统中的创新驱动领导者。

价值观

诚信、创新、专注、工匠精神。
