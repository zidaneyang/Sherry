About Us

Hong Kong Sherry International Co., LTD is a group company based in Hong Kong SAR, China, with its operations primarily focused on the global automotive sales ecosystem. We are dedicated to connecting the automotive industry chain, building a robust global marketing network, and becoming a significant international player in automotive sales.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower Chinese automotive brands to expand their presence in overseas markets through strategic partnerships, local sales network development, and a strong focus on new energy vehicle exports.

Core Business Strategies

  • Brand Representation: Establishing and promoting Chinese automotive brands in international markets.
  • End-to-End Distribution: Building comprehensive sales and distribution networks to support our partners' growth.
  • New Energy Vehicles: Focusing on the export and global promotion of environmentally-friendly vehicles.

Our Vision

We envision a future where Sherry International becomes a leading force in the global automotive industry, driving innovation and sustainability across the automotive value chain.

关于我们

香港 Sherry 国际有限公司是一家总部位于中国香港特别行政区的集团公司，其主要业务聚焦于全球汽车销售生态系统。我们致力于连接汽车产业链，建立强大的全球营销网络，成为汽车销售领域的重要国际参与者。

我们的使命

我们的使命是通过战略合作伙伴关系、当地销售网络的发展，以及对新能源汽车出口的高度重视，助力中国汽车品牌拓展海外市场。

核心业务策略

  • 品牌代理: 在国际市场上建立并推广中国汽车品牌。
  • 端到端分销: 构建全面的销售和分销网络，支持我们合作伙伴的增长。
  • 新能源汽车: 专注于环保汽车的出口和全球推广。

我们的愿景

我们展望未来，Sherry 国际将成为全球汽车行业的领先力量，在整个汽车价值链中推动创新和可持续发展。
