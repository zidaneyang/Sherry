About Us

Sherry International is a leading group company specializing in the global automotive sales ecosystem, headquartered in Hong Kong SAR, China. Our mission is to connect the automotive industry chain and build a robust global marketing network, aiming to become a key international player in automotive sales. With established marketing networks across multiple global markets, Sherry International is rapidly expanding its reach.

Core Businesses

  • Vehicle Export
  • Overseas Auto Dealerships

Vision, Mission, Values

At Sherry International, we are committed to creating a pioneering ecosystem for overseas automotive marketing, setting the standard for Chinese auto sales abroad.

Vision

To be an innovation-driven leader in the overseas automotive marketing ecosystem.

Mission
